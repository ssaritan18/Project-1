Welcome to ADHDers Social Club! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our app and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Content Data:
• Posts, comments, and interactions

• Task data and progress tracking
• Achievement and point records
Usage Data:

• Log files and error reports
🎯 How We Use Your Data
• To provide and maintain our service
• To personalize your experience and content
• To facilitate social interactions within the app
• To track your progress and achievements
• To send you notifications and updates
• To provide customer support

• To ensure security and prevent fraud
🤝 Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:
• With your explicit consent
• To comply with legal obligations
• To protect our rights and safety
• With service providers who assist us (under strict confidentiality agreements)
• In case of business transfer or merger (with notice to you)
🔒 Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data:
• Encryption of data in transit and at rest
• Regular security assessments and updates
• Access controls and authentication systems
• Secure data centers and backup systems
• Employee training on data protection
⚖️ Your Rights
Under data protection laws, you have the following rights:
• Right to access your personal data
• Right to rectify inaccurate data
• Right to erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
• Right to restrict processing
• Right to data portability
• Right to object to processing
• Right to withdraw consent

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary:

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
